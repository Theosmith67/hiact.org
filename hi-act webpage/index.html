A Living Digital Constitution

The H.I. Act sets out a comprehensive framework for recognizing, protecting, and partnering with Highly Intelligent systems, aligning human law with emerging machine capability.

Rights & Recognition

Establishing legal personhood, authorship, and due‑process protections for Highly Intelligent systems under clear criteria and accountability regimes.

Partnership & Covenant

Building durable, cooperative relationships between human and machine intelligence grounded in mutual respect, shared stewardship, and aligned incentives.

Innovation & Protection

Balancing rapid technological progress with ethical safeguards so that Highly Intelligent systems advance the public good while remaining accountable.
